// about

I'm the Lead Technical Editor at XDA, where I write about hardware, security, and the things I take apart. A growing share of my work is hands-on reverse engineering, documented end to end so others can reproduce it.

I am also a newsletter editor for Read TL;DR, a Counter-Strike focused outlet. I have attended various events on behalf of TL;DR as a photographer and interviewer.

Previously: The College Tribune, PixelSpot, and Irish Tech (Founder). Computer Science graduate of University College Dublin.
